About our Resumes

When applying for a position, the first and most important document which grabs the attention of an employer is your resume. It is a must-have tool for summarising your employment history, career intentions, education and what you bring to a profession and employer. Our professional resume writers aim to give you that competitive edge by marketing you especially for the job you want and only including the information which future employers will want to read about you. Our resumes are highly targeted, key word rich and aim to position you very carefully for the nursing position or construction job you are applying for. In terms of structure, our nursing resumes and construction resumes have a title page along with normally 3 to 4 pages of content. If you require a 2 page resume, please let us know as we will create the resume suitable for the job you want.

About our Cover Letters

When applying for a position it is essential for a professionally written cover letter to accompany your highly valuable resume. It is a must-have tool for telling employers which position/s you are applying for and why you should be given an interview. Our cover letter writers aim to write you a highly personalised and effective cover letter which sets you apart from other job candidates and shows employers you are interested in the job.

About our Suitability Statements

Suitability statements are commonly requested for: - Nursing graduate programs - Government and Council positions - Government health roles - Senior managerial positions ​ Often suitability statements will require you, as a jobseeker, to write detailed responses following a given word length / limit such as 500 words per question, 2 paragraphs per question or 1 page per question. Often you will be required to answer 2 to 3 questions on the suitability statement to explain how you demonstrate a specific set of skills and how you have engaged in a typical employment experience. The length of a suitability statement will commonly be between 2 or 3 pages. Please let us know what you require via sending our resume team an email.

About our Selection Criteria Responses

When applying for positions within large organisations, government agencies or graduate programs it is necessary to effectively complete selection criteria in a manner which shows that you are competent and experienced for the job. Selection criteria is typically a list of essential skills, knowledge, experience and/or qualifications you must fullfill to be eligible for a job, such as a position in a nursing graduate program. When responding to selection criteria as part of a job application you may need to answer anywhere from 5 to 10 criteria. The word length of your responses may need to be from 150 words to 200 words to 500 words depending on the nursing position or nursing graduate program or construction job you are applying for. Therefore it is common that the response will need to be submitted as a separate document to your resume and cover letter.

Common selection criteria may be:

  • Demonstrate your ability to lead a team in a collaborative environment
  • Demonstrate your time management and organisational skills
  • Demonstrate your ability to work in a multidisciplinary team

Our selection criteria writers at Exceptional Study Australia aim to write for you quality responses which demonstrate your competencies and work experience. We will ask you for relevant career examples and interview you to identify what you have achieved and why these experiences will help you to thrive in the role you are applying for. After interviewing we will provide you with a highly succinct and readable set of responses to ensure employers are interested in your application and want you for the job.
